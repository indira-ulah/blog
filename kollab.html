Inside Kollab

On June 10, 2025, we, the BSCS students from WMSU, had a very interesting visit to Kollab in Mandaluyong City. This trip was all about learning how tech companies work in the real world.

My Personal Insights

Visiting Kollab was a great experience. I really liked learning about how AI is used in practical ways, not just in theory. As a Frontend Developer and UI Designer, Miguel Siriban's tips on building a portfolio were super helpful.

It showed me that it's important to not only have technical skills but also be able to show your creativity and how you think. The whole visit made me feel more ready and excited for my future in the tech world.

What This Tour Mean To Us

This visit was more than just seeing a tech company; it was a chance to dive into a world of new ideas. We got to ask questions and imagine our future in the fast-changing world of computing.

WMSU CCS is thankful to Kollab for sharing their knowledge and inspiring the next generation of tech pros. This experience will help us be ready for the digital future!
