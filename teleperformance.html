Insde Teleperformance

On June 10, 2025, we, the BSCS students from WMSU, visited Teleperformance (TP) Philippines at SM Aura Office in Taguig City. This trip was exciting because TP is a big global company that helps businesses with customer services.

Ms. Beatrice Grace Bonjibod, a Brand Marketing Specialist, welcomed us. She explained that TP does more than just phone calls; they use new tech and focus on people to offer many services. She also shared that over half of their workers are women, which shows they care about equality.

We also learned that "soft skills" like talking well, working with others, being understanding, and flexible are very important for any job. After the talk, we toured TPâ€™s Innovation and Solutions Hub, where we saw cool new tech like automation and data analysis that makes business work better.

My Personal Insights

My visit to Teleperformance was really eye-opening. I saw how much technology is used in customer service, not just simple call centers. As a future tech professional, it was cool to see how they use automation and data to make things run smoothly.

The focus on soft skills also stuck with me. It's not just about coding; it's about being a good communicator and team player. This trip showed me how important it is to have both tech skills and people skills in the real world.

What This Tour Mean To Us

This visit really helped us understand Teleperformance's big role in global services. It made us want to do our best, be creative, and include everyone in our future careers.

We even got cool gifts from IBPAP, which reminded us of the awesome jobs waiting for us in the IT-BPM industry. This trip inspired us to be excellent and ready for the future!
