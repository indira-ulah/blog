About The Tour

The BSCS Educational Tour 2025 was an immersive week-long journey through some of the most prestigious tech companies in the Philippines, followed by a relaxing retreat in Tagaytay and Baguio.

Our journey commenced with a City Tour on the first day, setting the stage for the exciting week ahead. From June 9-11, we visited six leading companies: Foundever, Google, Teleperformance, Kollab, Asian Development Bank (ADB), and MicroSourcing, gaining valuable insights into the tech industry and potential career paths.

The final two days were spent in Tagaytay and Baguio, where we reflected on our experiences while enjoying the scenic beauty of these popular destinations.

Our Speakers

Insights from industry leaders and experts

Carlo Quezon

Recruitment Marketing and Sourcing Manager / Foundever

Carlo Quezon brings an inspiring message of resilience and opportunity. He believes in the power of seizing chances, even in the face of adversity. Carlo will share his personal philosophy, encapsulated by the saying, "Stand up and leave the crowd," encouraging the youth to carve their own paths and embrace unique opportunities for growth and success in their careers.

Atty. Yves Gonzales

Government Affairs and Public Policy / Google

Atty. Yves Gonzales take attendees on a captivating journey through the inspiring story of Google. He delve into the foundational principles and innovative spirit that propelled Google to become a global technology giant, offering insights into its growth, culture, and impact.

Beatrice Grace Bonjibod

Brand Marketing Specialist / Teleperformance

Beatrice Grace Bonjibod offer a unique look into the operational dynamics and inclusive culture at Teleperformance. She particularly highlight the significant presence of women in their workforce and discuss the environment that fosters their success and contributions within the company.

Ms. Jazmine Claire Calma

Senior Engineer, Quality Assurance / Kollab

Ms. Jazmine Claire Calma share her inspiring experience of working at Kollab, emphasizing the company's unique culture of freedom and fun. She discuss how Kollab fosters an environment that encourages creativity, work-life balance, and active participation in outdoor activities, making it an engaging and rewarding workplace.

Mr. Toni-Jan Keith Monserrat

Senior Principal Engineer AI & Data / Kollab

Mr. Toni-Jan Keith Monserrat provide a dual perspective, combining his expertise in algorithms with profound personal anecdotes. He did not only share insights into the technical aspects of AI and data but also deliver inspiring stories from his life journey, demonstrating the power of perseverance and dedication.

Mr. Miguel Siriban

UI/UX Designer / Kollab

Mr. Miguel Siriban, a seasoned UI/UX Designer, guide participants through the art of crafting interactive and compelling portfolios. He share practical design principles and creative approaches to help attendees showcase their skills and projects effectively, making a lasting impression.

Mr. Matt Farley

Infrastructure & Technology Director / Asia's Development Bank

Mr. Matt Farley share his delightful experiences of working in the Philippines, affirming the popular slogan "It's more fun in the Philippines" through his professional and personal anecdotes. He also highlight successful projects, including their impactful solar panel initiatives in the provincial regions, showcasing how technology can drive positive change in communities.

Ms. Aira Omalin

Business & Talent Intelligence Advisor / Microsourcing

Ms. Aira Omalin provide invaluable guidance on leveraging LinkedIn for professional success. She share practical tips and strategies on how to create a presentable and impactful LinkedIn profile, helping attendees optimize their online presence for career advancement and networking.

Ms. Heidee C. Enriquez

CEO / Microsourcing

As the CEO of Microsourcing, Ms. Haidee C. Enriquez share her remarkable and inspiring journey of building the company from the ground up. She offer insights into the challenges, triumphs, and key decisions that shaped Microsourcing into the successful enterprise it is today, providing valuable lessons in entrepreneurship and leadership.
